International Certifications

LDAB proudly prepares dancers for internationally recognised ballet qualifications through prestigious examination boards, giving every student a pathway towards technical excellence and artistic growth.

UNESCO – CID

CID is the International Dance Council, the official umbrella organisation for dance recognised by UNESCO.

LDAB proudly offers internationally recognised Teacher Training programmes certified by CID, connecting students and educators with a worldwide network spanning more than 170 countries.

CSTD

The Commonwealth Society of Teachers of Dancing is one of the world's leading dance organisations with over eighty-five years of excellence.

Through internationally recognised examinations and progressive training, LDAB students receive structured development while building confidence, artistry and performance skills.

Explore More

ISTD

The Imperial Society of Teachers of Dancing (ISTD), London, is one of the world's most respected dance examination boards. Its Imperial Classical Ballet syllabus develops strong technique, musicality, artistry and performance quality.

Students at LDAB receive internationally recognised training that prepares them for examinations, ballet awards, performance competitions and advanced professional opportunities.

Explore More

Royal Academy of Dance (RAD)

Founded in London in 1920, the Royal Academy of Dance is one of the world's leading organisations dedicated to classical ballet education.

RAD examinations develop exceptional technical standards, artistry and discipline while providing internationally recognised qualifications for aspiring dancers.

Explore More

Jason Winters Contemporary Technique

In partnership with the Commonwealth Society of Teachers of Dancing, LDAB proudly offers the internationally acclaimed Jason Winters Contemporary Technique programme.

This contemporary syllabus develops strength, versatility, musical interpretation and expressive movement, complementing classical ballet training while broadening every student's artistic capabilities.
